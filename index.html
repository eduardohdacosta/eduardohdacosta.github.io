En Breve

My name is Eduardo DaCosta, and I am a student of marketing and information systems at the University of Florida under the Warrington College of Business Administration. I am minoring in computer science and mass communications to specialize my area of study.

My professional interests include brand management and consulting with a heavy focus on information technology and graphic and web design. I am actively pursuing the skills required to excell in each career, the market-mindedness necessary for effective and influential decision making, and the personality that makes every action I take or project I take on unique to my talents and my ideas.

While I enthusiastically seek learning in a classroom setting, I also take every opportunity to learn through the experiences I receive as a result of my involvement on the UF campus. From actively advancing brotherhood in a professional fraternity to consulting with local Gainesville businesses to organizing college-wide events for the students of my college, I very much enjoy the parts I play in the organizations I am passionate about furthering.

Aside from that, I draw inspiration and enjoyment from a number of different outlets:
-The community driven works of John and Hank Green (Vlogbrothers)
-The daily articles posted from Advertising Age, WSJ, and Mashable.com
-A sizeable collection of TED talks
-The social media outlets I maintain from Twitter to GitHub to Tumblr
-The piano sitting in my living room
-My faith and relationship with Jesus Christ
-The proudly quirky family and motivated group of peers with whom I consistently surround myself

I look forward to connecting with you in the near future.
