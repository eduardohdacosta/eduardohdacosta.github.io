 About Us

The Business Administration College Council (BACC) is the governing body of student organizations within the Warrington College of Business Administration. It acts as the umbrella group for 30 organizations, providing them with funding, marketing advice, professional development resources, consulting for events and programming, and networking. Presidents and treasurers of each organization are required to attend meetings, held on the first Monday of every month (see calendar) at 6 pm.

We strive to increase the co-sponsorship of events between student organizations in the WCBA, to enhance the use of our financial planning services for our student organizations, to expand the variety of and opportunities for community building events and consistently host these programs throughout the year, and to develope further the business organizations' presence on the University of Florida campus.
